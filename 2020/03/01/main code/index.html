About me:

I am very interested in the question that what I am. In our cognitive scope, man and cosmos are the most super complex system. I believe that there is some mysterious connection between man and cosmos, so I am keen to explore the man, society, cosmos and the relationships among them. In the current world, we have the ability to record human behaviour data. Based on big data and statistical science, I study the laws and mechanisms behind human society from the perspective of social physics and network science. In the future, the computational social science will make remarkable achievements which let us have a deeper understanding of ourselves and the Creator. If the answer to what I am can be found, I will have no regrets in my life.
